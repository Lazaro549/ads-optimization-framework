# Display Ads Optimization Case

## Objective

Increase conversion efficiency while maintaining traffic volume.

Primary KPI: Improve ROAS  
Constraint: Do not increase total budget

---

## Initial Metrics (Baseline)

Impressions: 120,000  
Clicks: 3,600  
CTR: 3.0%  
CPC: $0.80  
Conversions: 72  
Conversion Rate: 2.0%  
Revenue: $4,320  
Ad Spend: $2,880  
ROAS: 1.5  
ACOS: 66%

---

## Diagnosis

- CTR was healthy → creative relevance acceptable
- Conversion rate was low → landing misalignment suspected
- High ACOS due to low CVR

Primary Issue:
Conversion Efficiency bottleneck

---

## Optimization Actions

1. Refined audience targeting (excluded low-intent segments)
2. Adjusted creative messaging to emphasize offer clarity
3. Improved landing page consistency with ad message
4. Paused lowest-performing creative variation

---

## Results (After 30 Days)

Impressions: 105,000  
Clicks: 3,150  
CTR: 3.0%  
CPC: $0.75  
Conversions: 126  
Conversion Rate: 4.0%  
Revenue: $7,560  
Ad Spend: $2,362  
ROAS: 3.2  
ACOS: 31%

---

## Key Insight

Improving conversion efficiency has stronger profitability impact than increasing traffic volume.

Optimization priority:
Conversion before scale.
