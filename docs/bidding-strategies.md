Bidding determines visibility, cost control, and scalability.

---

## Strategic Approaches

### 1. Growth Strategy
- Higher acceptable ACOS
- Focus on impression share
- Aggressive bid positioning

Used for:
- Product launches
- Market expansion

---

### 2. Profitability Strategy
- Strict ACOS or ROAS target
- Controlled bid adjustments
- Focus on high-margin segments

Used for:
- Stable products
- Margin protection

---

### 3. Hybrid Strategy
- Testing campaigns with flexible bids
- Scaling campaigns with strict targets

---

## Smart Budget Allocation

- Allocate more budget to high ROAS segments
- Reduce spend on high ACOS campaigns
- Separate testing budget from scaling budget

Never optimize bids without analyzing conversion rate.
