# Learning Roadmap – Performance & Marketplace Advertising

This roadmap reflects structured growth in performance marketing.

---

## Level 1 – Marketplace Foundations

- Understand ACOS logic
- Learn auction-based ranking
- Interpret impressions vs clicks vs sales
- Analyze direct vs assisted sales

---

## Level 2 – Display Optimization

- Improve CTR through creative testing
- Diagnose conversion inefficiencies
- Apply structured budget control
- Understand audience segmentation

---

## Level 3 – Strategic Bidding

- Define target ACOS by margin
- Model break-even point
- Apply scaling thresholds
- Control campaign profitability

---

## Level 4 – Performance Systems Thinking

- Portfolio-level optimization
- Multi-campaign architecture
- Predictive efficiency modeling
- Automated bid logic frameworks

---

Principle:
Sustainable growth = Visibility + Efficiency + Controlled Cost
