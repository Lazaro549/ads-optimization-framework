# Paid Media & Marketplace Glossary

---

## ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sales)
Ad Spend / Revenue  
Primary efficiency metric in marketplace advertising.

---

## ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
Revenue / Ad Spend  
Inverse of ACOS.

---

## Ad Rank
Auction-based score that determines ad position.

Typically influenced by:
- Bid strategy
- Quality indicators
- Expected performance

---

## Second-Price Auction
Winning advertiser pays the minimum required to beat the second competitor.

---

## Direct Sales
Purchases made immediately after ad click.

---

## Assisted Sales
Purchases made after ad interaction but on a different product.

---

## Traffic Quality
Alignment between targeting and purchase intent.

---

## Conversion Efficiency
Ability to convert traffic into revenue.

---

## Budget Control
Strategic allocation of spend based on performance tiers.
